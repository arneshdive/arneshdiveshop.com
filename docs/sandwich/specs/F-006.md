# F-006: Product variants (size, color) with individual pricing

> **Module:** Product Management · **Priority:** 6/100 · **Depends on:** F-005 · **Source:** prd.md:44

Products support variants such as size and color, each with its own price.

## Scope

**In:**
- Variant CRUD (size, color) attached to a product
- Per-variant pricing (integer cents)

**Out:**
- Variant-level stock tracking

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] **AC1** — Admin can add variants (size/color) to a product with their own price
- [ ] **AC2** — Customer can select a variant on the product detail page and see its price
