# F-005: Product CRUD (images, status, availability, featured)

> **Module:** Product Management · **Priority:** 12/100 · **Depends on:** F-001 · **Source:** prd.md:43-50

Admin product create/update/delete with image upload to Vercel Blob, active/inactive status, availability flag (no stock quantity), and featured-product selection.

## Scope

**In:**
- Product create/update/delete with Vercel Blob image upload
- Active/inactive status toggle
- Availability flag (available/unavailable) — no stock quantity tracking
- Featured-product selection for homepage/admin

**Out:**
- Stock/inventory quantity tracking (explicitly dropped by client)
- Product reviews/ratings

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] **AC1** — Admin can create a product with images, price (integer cents), category, and brand
- [ ] **AC2** — Admin can edit and soft-delete a product
- [ ] **AC3** — Admin can toggle a product's active/inactive and availability flags
- [ ] **AC4** — Admin can mark a product as featured
