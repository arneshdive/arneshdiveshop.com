# F-022: Transactional email notifications (Mailgun)

> **Module:** Email Notifications · **Priority:** 9/100 · **Depends on:** F-002, F-013, F-015 · **Source:** prd.md:150-155

Order confirmation, payment received, order shipped (with tracking), welcome, and password reset emails sent via Mailgun.

## Scope

**In:**
- Order confirmation email
- Payment received email
- Order shipped email with tracking number
- Welcome email for new registrations
- Password reset email

**Out:**
- SMS notifications
- Push notifications

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] **AC1** — Customer receives an order confirmation email after checkout completes
- [ ] **AC2** — Customer receives a payment-received email when payment is confirmed
- [ ] **AC3** — Customer receives a shipped email containing the tracking number when status moves to shipped
- [ ] **AC4** — New registrants receive a welcome email
- [ ] **AC5** — Password reset emails are sent via Mailgun (delivery already covered by F-004's OTP send)
