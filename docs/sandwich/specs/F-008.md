# F-008: Product CRUD with variants

> **Module:** Products · **Priority:** 13/100 · **Depends on:** F-002, F-006, F-009 · **Source:** prd.md:products-module

Full product management with variants, pricing in cents, slug generation, featured flag, ISR revalidation

## Scope

**In:**
- Product list view with filters (category, brand, status)
- Product create/edit form
- Product variants management (add, edit, remove)
- Image upload via Vercel Blob
- Price input in dollars, storage in cents
- Automatic slug generation from name
- Featured product flag
- Active/inactive toggle
- ISR revalidation on product changes
- Product detail page for storefront

**Out:**
- Inventory tracking
- Product reviews
- Related products

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] **AC1** — Admin can create product with name, slug, description, price, category, brand, images
- [ ] **AC2** — Admin can add variants with name, options, and optional price override
- [ ] **AC3** — Images upload to Vercel Blob and display in product gallery
- [ ] **AC4** — Price stored as cents, displayed as dollars in UI
- [ ] **AC5** — Featured products display on homepage
- [ ] **AC6** — Inactive products hidden from storefront
